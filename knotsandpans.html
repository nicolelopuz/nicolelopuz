Knots and Pans

A curated online gift registry for weddings, birthdays, baby showers and many other milestones.

Discovery and Exploration

Knots and Pans is a web based application made in wordpress. The user interface as well as the experience was chosen from a pre-made theme for ecommerce.

The client came to me with a list of problems and what she wanted to accomplish. Based on the list, I decided to gather and study the analytics of their site. I also tried navigating and creating my own registry. The site also is not mobile responsive ready. So I iterated on many variations of user flow and wireframes from creating a registry down to adding a gift, this gives us clarification on which in the list of be done first.

I've given her a set of wireframes to give her the idea of how it would look like and what the users would go through.

Based from the feedback we got from the wireframes, we decided to focus on adding gifts in the registry where a lot of users don't succeed. I created high-fidelity mockup variations in order to see which would be best for the users so we A/B test it for a week.

Results and Lessons

70% of guests who registered in the platform tried exploring and adding gifts to their cart. This is surely a success rate from the previous analytics that users are only pushed to add gifts to cart when they really need to checkout.

Solving things needs time and patience, the users are always right so carefully learn from them and base all decisions from the gathered data.
