Fashion 21 Cosmetics

A catalog website that shows all the Fashion 21 Cosmetics products available in the market and also all the promos, exciting event launches and reviews from famous endorsers and youtubers.

Visit Site

Context

Fashion 21 was born from the idea of fresh look for the new woman of the 21st century. It was to offer Filipinas a new brand of imported, good quality and long-lasting makeup at an affordable price.

Fashion 21 Cosmetics is a Filipino brand.

Problem

They recently rebranded their line and also want to modernize the style of their website. The client also wants a catalog of products where users can interact. A brand that wants a fresh, minimal and 21st century feel.

Timeline

Before and After look of website.
A sample wireframes of Checkout Process in Marketplace.

Strategy

Fashion 21 offers a vibrant and fresh vibe that fits young filipinas personalities. The brand style guide helped in creating a website that has the same aesthetics in order to have consistent identity design. Red was used as accents giving emphasis on the specific information or title.

Solution

My involvement was in giving Fashion 21 a complete overhaul website from their previous one. The challenge was to transform an old dated website into a fresh, trendy and fashion forward.
