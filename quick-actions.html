GlobeOne Quick Actions

Works GlobeOne Quick Actions

GlobeOne Quick Actions is a convenient access to that's most important. It's also a pre-solution to fixing the Information Architecture.

Principles

  • Frequently accessed tasks
  • Personalization (by brand)
  • Quick fix for GlobeOne’s IA
  • Customers need to feel secure about the information displayed and those that they share.
Interaction

Actions lead to screens or sections.

For new tasks, addition of new label.

When we tested the design with users, they loved ‘quick actions’: “they’re a benefit definitely!”


Best Practices
  • Choose links by most used and importance.
  • Keep the actions into a minimum of four. Try not to overwhelm with too much tasks.
  • Link directly to the task / weblink / deeplink / section.
Title Copy
  • Try to keep text short and concise. The maximum length of 15 characters or minimum supported size for four items.
  • Try to use verbs for quick actions title.
  • Write clear, simple, jargon-free title.
