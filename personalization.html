Fashion21 Cosmetics

Works Fashion21

A catalog website that shows all the Fashion 21 Cosmetics products available in the market and also all the promos, exciting event launches and reviews from famous endorsers and youtubers.

Context

Fashion 21 Cosmetics is a local drugstore make-up brand that can bought for a very reasonable price. It was born from the idea of fresh look for the new woman of the 21st century, offering Filipinas a new brand of imported, good quality and long-lasting makeup.

Problem

They recently rebranded their line and want to modernize their very own website. The client wants a catalog of products where users can interact by rating and reviewing products. In the long run, they also want to be an ecommerce platform but for this project they only want to showcase new products that they come out with.
