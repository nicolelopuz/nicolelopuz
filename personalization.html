GlobeOne Personalization

Works GlobeOne Quick Actions

Personalization targets experiences to users with the intent of delivering what they need when they need it.

Customer needs

  • To help customers maximize Globe promo or plan (data and plan right-sizing).
  • Gain customer loyalty and enhance engagement.
  • Send relevant notifications and messaging to customers.

User experience touchpoints

How to apply personalization?

Use case:

  • Explicitly tell users what we will do to his/her information.

Opportunity:

  • Information will be used for their own benefit and improve UX.

Targeted banners

Use case:

  • Customer is interested in music based from knowing our end-users.

Opportunity:

  • From the selected category and information we know, we can display targeted banners individually.

UX Metrics

Traffic

  • Looking at traffic to the content pushed to different profiles — are users reading or reacting to the content in expected ways?

Complaints

  • Keep track of complaints received from users that are related to personalization.

Up-to-date information

  • Review sources personalization data to make sure they are staying up-to-date and accurate. A personalization effort may have begun with a newly implemented or recently cleaned-up database, but may have not been updated ever since.

What works and doesn’t

  • Regular review of personalization efforts can enhance their usefulness over time.
